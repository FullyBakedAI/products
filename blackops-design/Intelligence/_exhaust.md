# Exhaust — Reusable Intake Patterns from BlackOps Stage 01

Captured as we go. Purpose: every pattern, question, tension, and template that emerges while running Intelligence on BlackOps itself becomes an input to a future **Intelligence-as-intake** product.

The instinct: the same Intelligence substrate that sharpens a company's strategy can also be the *onboarding for a product that adapts to the user*. BlackOps gives us the first end-to-end run at **company altitude**. What we learn here informs the product-altitude and experience-altitude versions downstream.

## What to capture

- **Questions that unlock disproportionate insight** — the ones where the answer reshapes a downstream decision. Candidates for "core intake" questions in any adaptive flow.
- **Questions that are filler** — plausible but the answer never changes what we do next. Candidates for removal.
- **Tension points** — where the Intelligence output contradicts the existing pitch. Signal; the pattern should surface them, not hide them.
- **Branches** — where an answer should fork the rest of the flow. Candidates for adaptive-intake logic.
- **Artefact shapes** — what the synthesised output looks like (problem statement, persona card, competitive map). Candidates for reusable schemas.
