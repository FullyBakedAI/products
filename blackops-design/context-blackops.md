## What It Is

An elite AI + Product Alpha Layer for Venture Capital. BlackOps functions as a **fund-embedded design and execution partner** — not a consultancy or vendor. We are a structural capability that VCs deploy across their portfolio. We work with early-stage companies at any stage to close the execution gap between what founders have and what the market demands.

**Target audience:** VC funds investing in early-stage businesses across any sector. The entry point is warm relationships with fund partners (e.g. Talos investor network, Anton intro). NY May 2026 is the first major pitch opportunity.

---

## The Model — Fund-Embedded, Not Vendor

BlackOps is proposed as a **standing partnership**, not a per-project engagement.

**How it works:**
- Fund identifies a high-conviction deal where the execution layer is weak (brand, product, story, org, AI)
- BlackOps is allocated into the round — fees come from the investment capital, zero cost to the fund
- BlackOps executes the full build alongside founders — typically 4–8 weeks
- BlackOps takes a small equity stake in the portfolio company
- Everything built and installed stays with the company — they own the operating system

**The alignment:** BlackOps only wins when the portfolio company wins. Skin in the game, not day rates.

**Long-term vision:** Build a track record of equity positions across winning companies. This is the path to a design-led fund — the fund without capital deployment now, proven returns via equity stakes, raise a fund later.

## The Problem: Engines Without Cockpits

Founders are fundable. Ideas are sound. Rounds are closing.

But founders rarely see — let alone budget for — the execution layer that determines whether a business actually wins:

VCs back them anyway. Then 18 months of runway disappears while founders figure it out.

That execution gap is where Adoption Alpha is destroyed.

## Alpha Studio

**Alpha Studio** is BlackOps's proprietary design/prototype phase tool. It is the BlackOps-exclusive version of the Fully Baked construction layer, used during the Design/Prototype phase of every engagement.

Key line: *"We run your vision through Alpha Studio before a single line of production code is written."*

- Not available to Fully Baked public users — exclusive to BlackOps engagements
- This is where vision becomes tangible: design and prototype happen as one motion
- The output of Alpha Studio is what gets handed to Build phase
- It IS the BlackOps Alpha Layer made real — the thing that de-risks every engagement before engineering spend
