## Identity
I'm a content creator — could be design, photography, fitness, cooking, tech reviews, whatever. My @handle IS my brand. I'm active on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and X at minimum. I might add Threads, Bluesky, Pinterest depending on my niche. Domain names are secondary — my audience finds me through social platforms, not websites. I might want a .com eventually but my social presence is what pays the bills.

## Goal
Find a handle that's available and consistent across every platform I use. Ideally short, memorable, no underscores or numbers. The same @name everywhere.

## Pain Points (by severity)
1. **Inactive accounts are squatting my name.** The handle I want is taken on Instagram by someone who posted once in 2019 and disappeared. I can't claim it without a trademark. This is infuriating.
2. **Every platform has different rules.** X doesn't allow periods, Instagram has a character limit, TikTok handles work differently. A name that works on one platform might not work on another.
3. **Inconsistent handles hurt my brand.** I'm @SamCreates on Instagram, @sam_creates on TikTok, @SamCreatesHQ on YouTube. My audience can't find me. It looks unprofessional.
4. **Adding numbers and underscores is embarrassing.** @samcreates99 screams "I couldn't get the real one." It undermines credibility.
5. **No way to know when a handle becomes available.** If that dormant account gets deleted, I'd want to claim it instantly. But there's no alert system.

## Workflow (How I Do It Today)
1. Think of a handle that represents my brand
2. Open Instagram — search for it — taken (inactive account)
3. Open TikTok — available!
4. Open YouTube — taken (12 subscribers, last video 2020)
5. Open X — available
6. Try variations: TheRealSam, SamCreatesHQ, Sam.Creates
7. Can't find any single variation that's free everywhere
8. Settle on a compromise — different handles on different platforms
9. Spend the next year explaining to followers where to find me on each platform

## What Good Looks Like
I type my desired handle and instantly see availability across all my key platforms. When it's taken somewhere, the tool tells me: is the account active or dormant? How long since it last posted? Is there any way to claim it? And give me variations that ARE available everywhere — not just random alternatives but handles that keep my brand identity. Bonus: alert me if a squatted handle becomes available.

## Red Flags (I'd Abandon Immediately If...)
- It focuses on domains and buries social handle checking — that's backwards for me
- It checks platforms I don't care about (eBay, PayPal, WordPress) and misses ones I do (Threads, Bluesky)
- It can't tell me if an account is active or dormant — "taken" isn't enough information
- The interface feels corporate or enterprise — I'm a creative, not a business analyst
- It doesn't let me check without creating an account

## Voice
Creative, visual, slightly frustrated. I care about aesthetics — how things look and feel matters to me professionally. I say things like "my handle is my brand", "I need to be @samcreates EVERYWHERE", "it's so annoying that someone with zero followers is sitting on my name." I'm active on social media so I'll share a tool that helps me — word of mouth is my currency. I'm less willing to pay for tools (creator budgets are tight) but I'll recommend tools to my audience if they're genuinely useful.
