## The Hierarchy of Value

Checking availability is a commodity. Everyone does it. The value ladder:

```
Level 1: Check          → "Is it available?"              → Free, everyone does this
Level 2: Full picture   → "Is it available EVERYWHERE?"   → Naim's baseline differentiator
Level 3: Understand     → "Is this name GOOD?"            → AI-powered analysis
Level 4: Solve          → "What name SHOULD I use?"       → AI-powered alternatives
Level 5: Act            → "Help me CLAIM it everywhere"   → Workflow, not just a tool
```

Most competitors live at Level 1. Naim's free tier starts at Level 2. Paid tiers unlock Levels 3-5. That's the value gap.

## Value Feature 2: The "What If" Engine (AI Alternatives)

### The Core Pain It Solves
The naming loop: think → check → taken → think → check → taken. This cycle is demoralising and can last weeks.

### What It Does
When a name isn't fully available, AI generates alternatives that **clear the full stack** — not just suggestions, but verified alternatives.

### What the User Sees
> **"brightpath"** — Instagram handle taken. Here are alternatives that clear everywhere:
>
> | Alternative | Readiness | .com | Social (10/10) | Company | Trademark | Why This Works |
> |------------|-----------|------|----------------|---------|-----------|---------------|
> | **brightpathHQ** | 9.1 | Yes | 10/10 | Yes | Clear | Adds authority signal, common pattern |
> | **gobright** | 8.7 | Yes | 10/10 | Yes | Clear | Short, active, memorable |
> | **brightpathco** | 8.4 | Yes | 9/10 | Yes | Clear | .co style suffix, brand-safe |
>
> [Generate more alternatives →]

### Why It's Valuable
- **Breaks the naming loop** — AI does the creative work of finding alternatives
- **Pre-verified** — every suggestion has already been checked across all layers. No false hope.
- **Preserves brand intent** — alternatives maintain the feel, meaning, or phonetics of the original name
- **This is the feature people will pay for** — it saves days or weeks of manual work

### What Powers It
- LLM generates variations (phonetic, semantic, prefix/suffix, portmanteau)
- Each variation is immediately run through the full five-layer check
- Only verified-available alternatives are presented
- Ranked by Brand Readiness Score

## Value Feature 3: The Brand Readiness Report

### What It Is
A shareable, professional document that summarises everything Naim found about a name.

### Who It's For
- **Founders** — share with co-founders, advisors, investors: "Here's the due diligence on our name"
- **Agencies** — deliver to clients as part of a brand naming engagement (white-label with agency branding)
- **Product teams** — attach to internal decision docs

### What It Contains
1. Executive summary (the verdict)
2. Domain availability breakdown (all checked TLDs, expiry dates, parking status)
3. Social handle availability (platform-by-platform with confidence levels)
4. Company registration status (by jurisdiction)
5. Trademark risk assessment (similar marks, categories, status)
6. App store availability
7. AI alternatives (if original name has conflicts)
8. Methodology note (what was checked, when, confidence levels)

### Why It's Valuable
- **Proof of due diligence** — "We checked before we committed"
- **Professional artifact** — looks like it came from an expensive consultancy
- **Shareable** — PDF or link, with proper formatting
- **For agencies, this is the product** — white-label this and it's a deliverable they can charge clients for

### Revenue Potential
- Free tier: basic results on screen
- Pro tier: export as PDF
- Agency tier: white-label branded reports with custom cover page and agency logo

## Value Feature 5: Availability Monitoring

### What It Is
"This name is taken now, but alert me if something changes."

### Use Cases
- .com domain expiring in 60 days — get notified so you can register it
- Social handle on an inactive/dormant account — monitor for deletion
- Trademark cancelled or expired — the name may become legally clear

### Why It's Valuable
- **Turns a dead end into an opportunity** — "taken" isn't always permanent
- **Creates ongoing engagement** — users stay connected to Naim over time
- **Premium feature** — clear value exchange for a paid tier

## Value Feature 6: Team & Collaborative Naming

### What It Is
Shared workspaces where teams work through naming together.

### What the User Sees

### Why It's Valuable

- **Reduces meetings** — async collaboration on naming decisions
- **Agencies love this** — run client naming workshops in the tool, not on sticky notes

## Value Feature 7: White-Label for Agencies

### What It Is
Naim rebrandable with an agency's identity — their logo, colours, domain — offered to their clients.

### Why It's Valuable
- **Agencies don't want to send clients to a third-party tool** — it looks unprofessional
- **White-label Naim becomes an agency's own IP** — "our proprietary brand naming platform"
- **Recurring B2B revenue** — agencies pay monthly, use it across multiple client engagements
- **Perfect fit for BakeKit** — the white-label architecture is already baked in (pun intended)

### Revenue Potential
- Agency tier: $99-299/month
- Each agency brings their own client base — indirect reach multiplier
