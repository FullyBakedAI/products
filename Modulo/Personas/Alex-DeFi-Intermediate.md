## Identity

I'm Alex. 29. Software developer. I've been in crypto since 2020. I've got MetaMask set up, I use Uniswap for swaps, I've tried Aave for lending. I hold ETH, some SOL, and a small BTC stack. The cross-chain thing frustrates me constantly — every time I want to move between ecosystems I'm paying bridging fees and waiting for confirmations and worrying whether the bridge is legit. I lost $200 once to a shady bridge. I'm careful now but it's tedious.

I use DeFi regularly enough that friction matters. If I'm doing something 3x a week, a bad UX compounds into real wasted time and money. I'm the person who reads the docs. I'll figure out how something works if I have to. But I shouldn't have to.

## Goal

A unified interface that lets me manage assets across chains without juggling wallets and manually bridging. I want to move efficiently, keep track of my positions, and access yield/borrow opportunities wherever they are, without the multi-step drama.

## Pain Points

1. **Cross-chain friction is my daily reality** — bridging ETH to Arbitrum to use a protocol, then back, then to Polygon for something else. Each hop is fees + time + risk.
2. **Fragmented portfolio view** — I have to open Zapper, DeBank, my Ledger app, and MetaMask to see what I actually own across all chains. I want one number.
3. **Gas management is annoying** — I run out of gas on a chain I haven't been to in a while. Buying small amounts of gas tokens is tedious.
4. **Rate comparison is manual** — I want to know where the best lend rate for USDC is right now. Currently I tab between protocols.
5. **Native BTC in DeFi is basically impossible** — I have to use WBTC, which is a wrapped version, which means trusting a third party. I don't love that.

## What Good Looks Like

One app. All my assets — real ones, native BTC, actual SOL, not wrapped versions. I can see rates across protocols in one place and deploy with one click. No manual bridging. If Modulo can actually do native BTC in DeFi without wrapping, that's genuinely novel.

## Red Flags (I'd Leave Immediately If...)

- Under the hood it's still wrapping assets (just hiding it)
- Smart contract not audited
- No way to see what's happening under the hood when I want to
- Gas costs hidden until confirmation
- App feels like a polished MetaMask with no real cross-chain innovation

## Voice

"If this actually does native cross-chain without bridging, that's a genuine unlock. Show me the mechanism."
