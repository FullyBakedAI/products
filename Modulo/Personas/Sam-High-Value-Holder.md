## Identity

I'm Sam. 44. Finance background — spent 12 years in institutional trading before going independent. I hold a significant BTC position ($800k+), some XRP, and a reasonable ETH stack. I got into crypto early, made good decisions, held through volatility. My problem now is that this capital is largely idle. I understand yield. I understand collateralisation. What I don't understand is why accessing DeFi protocols with my BTC requires so many trust assumptions and intermediaries.

I've looked at institutional DeFi products. They're either not ready, too slow to onboard, or require KYC processes that take weeks. I'm comfortable with self-custody. I'm not comfortable with opacity.

## Goal

Access liquidity against my BTC position without selling. Borrow USDC against BTC collateral, deploy it, earn. This is a standard tradfi concept — using assets as collateral — and it should work in crypto without me needing to wrap my BTC and trust a custodian.

## Pain Points

1. **Native BTC collateral doesn't really exist in DeFi** — WBTC requires trusting BitGo as a custodian. That's a real risk I'm not comfortable ignoring.
2. **Borrow rates are opaque** — I want to understand the rate mechanism before I commit capital, not discover it post-transaction.
3. **Liquidation mechanics are scary** — in TradFi I get a margin call. In DeFi I get liquidated with no warning if I miss a price move. I need to understand and manage this.
4. **Portfolio health visibility** — if I'm borrowing against collateral, I need a real-time dashboard showing LTV, liquidation threshold, and buffer. Not buried in a sub-screen.
5. **Smart contract risk** — what are these contracts? Are they audited? By whom? What's the TVL? These are due diligence questions I'd ask about any investment.

## What Good Looks Like

A product that respects my sophistication. Shows me the mechanics. Gives me the data I need to make informed decisions. If I can borrow against native BTC at a competitive rate with transparent liquidation mechanics and a real audit trail — that's genuinely useful at scale.

## Red Flags (I'd Leave Immediately If...)

- "Trustless" claims without technical evidence
- No audit reports visible
- Borrow rates not explained
- Liquidation process not documented
- Company has no legal/regulatory clarity
- The only team info I can find is anonymous

## Voice

"I'm not looking for a better MetaMask. I'm looking for DeFi prime brokerage. Does this exist yet?"
